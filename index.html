Our Story

Where Royal Flavours
Meet Nature's Embrace

Founded in 2020, Royal Kitchen Blue is North Bengal's first and largest cloud kitchen — a women-owned culinary sanctuary nestled in the heart of Sherpur. What started as a passion for authentic flavours has grown into Sherpur's most beloved dining destination.

Our open-air garden setting, warm lights, and lush greenery create a magical atmosphere for every occasion — from intimate family dinners to grand corporate celebrations. Every dish is crafted by our internationally certified Executive Chef, using the finest ingredients and time-honoured recipes.

The Culinary Mind Behind RKB

Meet Executive Chef
Yeasin Ali Himel

Yeasin Ali Himel

Executive Chef & Founder · Royal Kitchen Blue

Chef Yeasin is the heart and soul of Royal Kitchen Blue. With a passion for authentic flavours rooted in Bengali culinary heritage, he has transformed a simple dream into North Bengal's most celebrated cloud kitchen.

His rigorous international training and multiple industry awards reflect a commitment to culinary excellence that shines through every dish served at RKB.

Qualifications & Awards
